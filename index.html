DoubleZ's Particle Simulator

By: Zara Shipchandler and Zirou Zhang

Magnetism

Click to have your cursor act as a magnet, otherwise the particles will repel the cursor.

Galaxy

Move the cursor to see the galaxy spin. You can also zoom in/out and drag to see it in 3D.

Ripples

Click to see colorful ripples appear on the screen starting from the cursor position.

Screen Saver

Click and drag the cursor to have the particles follow.

Fountain

Simulation of a flowing fountain originating from the center of the screen.

Sprinkler

Move your cursor to see the sprinkler follow and click to see a color change.

Explosion

Watch a green explosion of millions of generated particles.

Fireworks

Click to see a firework launch and explode at your cursor position.
